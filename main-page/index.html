What we do

HackStudio was founded on three pillars.

Redefining Programming

Learn valuable skills to not only solve problems and accomplish tasks, but also to jump into the world of competitive programming and build your own projects. 

For Students, By Students

Sometimes, instructors can be hard to connect with. However at HackStudio, you'll coached by a peer mentor, who understands your struggles and knows the process.

Tailored Learning

No matter what level of experience you are coming from, you can expect a process perfectly fit for your skillset, to teach you from the ground up.

Frequently ASked questions

Are mentors paid?

Our mentors are volunteers who work on their own time to help run this program. Mentors can earn volunteer hours through our program. 

What is the mentor to student ratio?

We expect to pair one or two mentors with a group of 3-5 students to have a mix of personalized guidance along with collaborative activity.

Who can we reach out to for more information?

Feel free to email abhi.nadella@gmail.com or call/text 518-384-5760 if you have any questions. You can also reach out at the support channels listed in our Contact page.

About US

The Story of HackStudio

 HackStudio was founded by two high-schoolers, Joyal Mathew and Abhi Nadella. Both of them had a passion and drive for computer science and wanted to share that with the world. Seeing how many students in underprivileged communities did not have access to learning resources or opportunities, the two devised a plan to foster interest by taking a new approach: encouraging students to pursue computer science through the lens of competitive programming. Thus, HackStudio was born.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to help students utilize their potential through programming.
